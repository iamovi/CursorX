Intro

Simply change your normal cursor to Cool Cursor.

Welcome to CursorX, a tool that allows you to easily transform your ordinary cursor into something extraordinary. With CursorX, you can personalize your cursor to reflect your style and creativity. Whether you prefer a sleek and minimal design or something more vibrant and eye-catching, CursorX has got you covered.

Getting started with CursorX is a breeze. Just follow our simple installation guide, and you'll be on your way to enjoying a customized cursor experience. Feel free to explore the various options and features available, and make your cursor truly unique.

CursorX is open source and hosted on GitHub. We welcome contributions from the community, so if you have ideas for improvements or new features, don't hesitate to get involved. Check out our GitHub repository to stay updated and contribute to the project.

Installation

To use CursorX >

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/cursorx@0.0.4/cursorx-style.css">



Simply copy the tag and paste it into your HTML head tag.



And >

<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/cursorx@0.0.4/cursorx.js"></script>



Copy the tag and paste it into your HTML at the end of the body tag.

Congrats! You successfully installed CursorX on your project, Now to implement cursor effect >


<div class="CursorX-dot-type1" data-cursor-dot></div> <div class="CursorX-outline-type1" data-cursor-outline></div>





Simply copy this code and paste it to anywhere of your html body tag. And BOOM you are all set to enjoy CursorX.


To implement another effect, just change type1 to type 2 >

<div class="CursorX-dot-type2" data-cursor-dot></div> <div class="CursorX-outline-type2" data-cursor-outline></div>





Quick Start


            <!DOCTYPE html>
            <html lang="en">
              <head>
                <meta charset="UTF-8" />
                <meta name="viewport" 
                content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0" />
                <title>Document</title>
            
                <link rel="stylesheet" 
                href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/cursorx@0.0.4/cursorx-style.css">    
                <style>
                  button {
                      background-color: aquamarine;
                      cursor: pointer;
                      display: block;
                      border: 1px solid black;
                      margin: auto;
                      margin-top: 150px;
                      padding: 10px;
                  }
                  button:hover {
                    border-radius: 10px;
                  }
                </style>
              </head>
              <body>
                <!-- cursor -->
                <div class="CursorX-dot-type1" data-cursor-dot></div>
                <div class="CursorX-outline-type1" data-cursor-outline></div>
            
                <!-- cursor -->
                <!-- <div class="CursorX-dot-type2" data-cursor-dot></div>
                <div class="CursorX-outline-type2" data-cursor-outline></div> -->
            
                <button>Test ME!</button>
            
                <script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/cursorx@0.0.4/cursorx.js"></script>
              </body>
            </html>
